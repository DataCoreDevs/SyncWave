ABOUT US

Your emotions, your music.

SyncWave is an AI-powered mood-based music recommendation platform that understands how you feel and adapts song suggestions accordingly. Instead of generic playlists, SyncWave analyzes listening behavior, tracks emotional patterns, and creates a more personal music experience.

FEATURES

MOOD ANALYSIS

Detects your current mood using listening behavior and smart recommendation logic.

TOMORROW PREDICTION

Predicts tomorrow's likely mood based on recent emotional trends.

MOOD HISTORY

Track how emotions change over time using intelligent mood history.

SMART RECOMMENDATIONS

Dynamic music suggestions based on detected moods.
